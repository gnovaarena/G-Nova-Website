F.A.Q

What is G. NOVA ARENA?
G. NOVA ARENA is a hybrid gaming and sports community that organises online gaming tournaments and offline sports events like football, cricket, basketball, and more.
Who can participate in your events?
Anyone aged 18 or above can register and participate in our events. We welcome gamers and athletes of all skill levels, from beginners to professionals.
How do I register for tournaments?
Registration is simple! Navigate to the Events page, click the Register button on the event page. It will redirect you to a Google Form where you must fill in your details and upload payment proof.
Are the events online or offline?
G. NOVA ARENA is a hybrid community. We organize both competitive online tournaments for digital gamers and offline events for field athletes (football, cricket, etc.). Check specific event details for location info.
Is there an entry fee?
Entry fees vary by tournament. Some community events are free to play, while major tournaments with cash prizes may require a registration fee. All details are listed on the individual event cards.
Do I need to create an account to join events?
No. Our website is partially static and does not support user account creation. All registrations are handled through Google Forms.
What information do I need to provide while registering?
You'll need to enter your name, age, gender, email, phone number or WhatsApp number, and upload your payment proof.
Where do your offline events happen?
Offline events are held at rented stadiums, clubs, or grounds. Event locations will be mentioned in each event's Google Form.
What platforms are used for online tournaments?
We host online gaming events on popular platforms and game titles. Specifics are mentioned in the event details.
How do I pay the entry fee?
Payments are made via a UPI QR code included in the Google Form. We do not use any payment gateway on our website.
Is my registration confirmed after payment?
Yes — once you submit the form and upload payment proof, your registration is considered complete.
Can I get a refund if I change my mind?
Refunds are only provided if an event does not meet the minimum team requirement. No refunds for voluntary cancellations or no-shows.
Do you sell or share my personal data?
No. We do not sell or share your data. It is securely stored in our Google account environment.
Are cookies used on this website?
No. Our website does not use any cookies or tracking technologies.
Are there rules for online events?
Yes. Cheating or exploiting in online events is strictly prohibited. All decisions made by the organisers are final.
What if I get injured during an offline event?
We assist in getting the injured participant to medical care, but we are not liable for treatment or hospital charges. Participation is at your own risk.
What kind of behavior is expected at events?
We expect fair play, respect, and discipline. Any violation, including rough play or misconduct, may lead to disqualification or a permanent ban.
How can I contact support?
You can reach us via email at contact@gnovaarena.in or join our Discord server for real-time support.
